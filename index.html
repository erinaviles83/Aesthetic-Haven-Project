Welcome to Aesthetic Haven!

Since becoming an esthetician in 2013, I have built a business that reflects who I am. Wanting each and every client to feel comfortable and welcomed. Knowing they will receive a stellar service every time and leave feeling better about whatever is on their mind. Of course, having plenty of laughs along the way!

I have met so many incredible people throughout my journey who have inspired me to be my best. My support system is undeniable. I try to pass along the same encouragement to others as I find we all need a sounding board, shoulder to cry on or just an ear to listen. I hope you find Aesthetic Haven to be your retreat, your safe place. I look forward to taking care of you for years to come!

My clients are my top priority. When it comes to products, ingredients, new technology or techniques, I strive to provide the best for them, with their best interest in mind. Working together, we can discuss products and services that fit your needs and lifestyle.

With love,
Erin
